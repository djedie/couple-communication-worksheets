# Yes / No / Maybe – Couple Communication Worksheet

For each item, answer honestly:

- Yes: I’m comfortable with this
- No: This doesn’t feel right for me
- Maybe: I’m curious, unsure, or open to discussing

There is no obligation to act on any answer.

The goal is conversation, not performance.
